**PIXELBUILD - Key Features**

**Core Gameplay**
- **Browser-based 2D sandbox MMO** with **real-time multiplayer** worlds.
- **Account system** (create/login/logout) with persistent progress.
- **Block place + break loop** with drops, stacking, and pickup.
- **Inventory + tools** (fist/wrench), plus equipment-based movement boosts.

**World Systems**
- Persistent tile worlds with **world locks**, owner/admin permissions, and door access control.
- Special blocks: **signs**, **weather machine**, **camera logs**, **spawn blocks**, **anti-gravity**, hazards.
- **Durability-based blocks**, separate **farmables**, and configurable **seed grow times**.

**Economy**
- **Vending machines**, **donation boxes**, and **storage chests**.
- Gambling machines: **Reme Roulette**, **Blackjack**, **Slots** (multiple versions).
- **Owner Tax Block**: world-wide tax on gambling machine earnings.
- **Splicing Machine** for seed crafting with ingredient recipes.
- **Gacha/Mystery Block** with weighted rewards (gems/items/cosmetics/titles).

**Progression**
- **Daily quests** + long-term quests with event-based tracking.
- **Achievement system** with persistent stats.
- **Quest World mode** with quest NPC block, one active path per world, and quest chains.
- Flexible rewards: gems, blocks, cosmetics, titles, tools.

**Cosmetics and Titles**
- Cosmetic slots: **shirts, pants, shoes, hats, wings, swords**.
- Shared render schema for easier cosmetic additions.
- Wings include **double jump** support.
- Advanced title styles: **glow**, **rainbow**, **gradient**, and mixed styles.

**Social and Multiplayer**
- **Global chat**, private messages (**/msg**, **/r**), and friend system.
- **Player profiles**, wrench interactions, and **player trading**.
- Discord verification flow integration.

**Admin and Security**
- Role-based admin system (**moderator/admin/manager/owner**).
- Dashboard-style admin panel with action console, logs, and audit trail.
- Anti-cheat anomaly logging and moderation commands.
- Backend-backed admin writes through **Cloudflare Worker gateway**.

**Tech and Quality**
- **Firebase Realtime Database** persistence and world sync.
- Background workers for **player sync** and **read processing**.
- JSON **backup/export/import** support.
- **Responsive UI** for desktop and mobile controls.
